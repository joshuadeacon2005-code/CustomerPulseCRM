### 📊 4. Analytics & Admin Dashboard Enhancements

**Files to edit:**

* `/src/components/Analytics/AnalyticsDashboard.tsx`
* `/server/controllers/analyticsController.ts`
* `/server/routes/analyticsRoutes.ts`

#### Metrics:

* **Total New Customers (month-to-date)**
* **Customer Interactions (month-to-date)**
* **Monthly Targets per Customer (sales tracking)**
* **Monthly Targets per Salesperson**

  * Visible only to that **salesperson** and roles above them (Manager, Admin, Director).

#### Behavior:

* **Salesperson view:**
