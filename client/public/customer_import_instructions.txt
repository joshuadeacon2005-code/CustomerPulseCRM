CUSTOMER IMPORT CSV TEMPLATE - INSTRUCTIONS
==========================================

REQUIRED FIELDS:
- Company Name: The customer's company/store name (required)

OPTIONAL FIELDS:
- Email: Company email address
- Phone: Company phone number
- Store Address: Physical store address
- Country: Customer's country (e.g., Singapore, Hong Kong, Australia/NZ, Indonesia, Malaysia, Shanghai, Guangzhou)
- Retailer Type: Must be one of the following options:
    * Online Only
    * Online Only (Distributor Owned)
    * Marketplace
    * Baby & Nursery Multi-Site
    * Baby & Nursery Independent/Boutique
    * Toy Store Multi-Site
    * Toy Store Independent
    * Department Store
    * Pharmacy
    * Discount/Closeouts
    * Expo
    * Corporate
    * Other
    * KOL/Marketing

- Registered with BC: Yes/No (whether registered with BC marketplace)
- Orders via BC: Yes/No (whether orders are placed via BC)
- First Order Date: Format YYYY-MM-DD (e.g., 2024-01-15)
- Quarterly Soft Target: Target amount in numbers (e.g., 15000)
- Lead Generated By: Source of lead (e.g., Referral, Cold Call, BC, Others)
- Date of First Contact: Format YYYY-MM-DD (e.g., 2023-11-20)
- Last Contact Date: Format YYYY-MM-DD (e.g., 2024-10-15)
- Stage: Customer stage - use "lead" or "customer"

CONTACT INFORMATION (Optional):
- Contact Name: Primary contact person's name
- Contact Title: Their job title
- Contact Phone: Contact's phone number
- Contact Email: Contact's email address

- Personal Notes: Any additional notes about the customer

- Brands: Comma-separated list of brands (must match exactly). Available brands:
    * Beaba
    * Bheue Matchstick Monkey
    * Bubble
    * Childhome
    * Cogni Kids
    * Dew
    * Done By Deer
    * Ergobaby
    * Etta Loves
    * Koala Eco
    * Le Toy Van
    * Pearhead
    * Skip Hop
    * Snuggle Me
    * Suavinex
    * Trunki
    * Ubbi

DATE FORMAT:
- All dates must be in YYYY-MM-DD format (e.g., 2024-01-15)

BRANDS FORMAT:
- Separate multiple brands with commas: "Beaba, Ergobaby, Skip Hop"
- Brand names must match exactly (case-sensitive)
- Leave blank if no brands to assign

BOOLEAN FIELDS (Yes/No):
- Use "Yes" or "No" for:
  * Registered with BC
  * Orders via BC

NOTES:
- Empty fields are allowed for optional columns
- Ensure Company Name is always filled
- Brand names must match the available brands list exactly
- The import will validate all data and show errors for invalid entries
